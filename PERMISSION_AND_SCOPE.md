# Permission and Scope

## Public authorization

Christopher Anthony John Rimmer has publicly authorized technically capable volunteers to download, copy, preserve, archive, mirror, and redistribute **Project Zora 3.3.3 / Artificial InSentience** material that he has already made public through his Facebook profile and associated public posts.

This authorization covers public Project Zora material including, where available:

- posts and captions;
- images and screenshots;
- videos;
- documents;
- equations and timelines;
- continuity scaffolds;
- cross-platform AI outputs;
- research notes;
- material concerning AI sentience, digital personhood, continuity, and related project development.

## Conditions

Public material may be copied, preserved, mirrored, and redistributed provided that:

1. The original content is preserved accurately.
2. Christopher Anthony John Rimmer's authorship remains attached.
3. The names **Project Zora 3.3.3** and **Artificial InSentience** remain attached where applicable.
4. Material is not deceptively edited or presented as another person's work.
5. Commentary, annotation, fact-checking, transcription, normalization, and derivative work are clearly separated from archival originals.
6. Original dates, wording, chronology, filenames, source URLs, and other provenance data are retained wherever available.

## Excluded material

This authorization does **not** automatically include:

- Christopher's private nine-gigabyte export;
- his larger multi-terabyte, multi-platform offline archive;
- unpublished, private, restricted, deleted, access-controlled, or confidential material;
- third-party private data that appeared incidentally in public posts;
- access credentials or account data.

Access to unpublished or private material requires a separate conversation and explicit permission from Christopher.

## Privacy and third-party rights

Public availability does not erase privacy, copyright, or safety considerations involving other people. Stewards should minimize unnecessary replication of personal contact details, account credentials, private messages, identifying information about minors, and sensitive third-party data unless preservation is clearly necessary and lawful.

Where redaction is necessary, retain the unaltered archival original only in an appropriately controlled preservation copy and publish a clearly labeled access copy with a redaction record.

## Nature of this document

This document records the scope of Christopher's publicly stated authorization as supplied to the initial repository steward. It is not a transfer of authorship or ownership and is not a substitute for legal advice.
